Dog Dog and their breeds About dogs

Dogs are one of the most beloved and popular pets worldwide, known for their loyalty, intelligence, and ability to form strong bonds with humans. Here's some key information about dogs: Species: Dogs belong to the species Canis lupus familiaris. They are domesticated mammals and a subspecies of wolves, having evolved alongside humans for thousands of years. Lifespan: The average lifespan of a dog is around 10-15 years, depending on the breed, health, and care they receive. Smaller breeds tend to live longer than larger breeds
